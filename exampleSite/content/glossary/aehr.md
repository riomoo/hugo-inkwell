Aehr was the capital of the {{< term term="Meridian Compact" >}}, a confederation of seagoing city-states that collapsed during the Silence of 1412. Its drowning is recorded in the *Annals of the Outer Shore* as sudden and total.

Cartographers mark its location with a footnote. Sailors report nothing but open sea.

Both of these things are simultaneously true and not true: Aehr exists, and does not exist, in the way that all drowned things continue to exist — as a shape the water holds, as a memory the sea keeps.

Its dead remain. They have shed weight and warmth over three centuries and become something between person and place. The city's continued existence as a haunt is maintained by the will of its harbor-warden, {{< char name="Veth" >}}.
