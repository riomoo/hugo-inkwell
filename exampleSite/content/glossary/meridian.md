The Meridian is the path between worlds — not a metaphor, but a literal body of water that exists in the space where two versions of the same coast fail to match up.

Most cartographers who encounter evidence of it assume a surveying error and correct their maps. A few draw it in pencil. Fewer still leave it in.

{{< callout type="lore" >}}
The scholar Marev of the Outer Shore wrote: *"There are places where the sea does not agree with itself. Where you sail east and arrive west. Where the stars are slightly wrong. I have been mapping these inconsistencies for twenty years and I believe they form a shape."* She did not live to complete the map. Her daughter is trying.
{{< /callout >}}

The Meridian is not a place you can sail to directly. It is a condition of the water — a state that certain stretches of sea enter under circumstances that are not fully understood. The dead city of Aehr sits at one of its anchor points.

Opening the Meridian requires a key. The nature of that key is a matter of some debate among the very few people who know it exists.
