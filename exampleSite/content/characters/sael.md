Sael is the daughter of a cartographer who spent her life searching for the {{< term term="Meridian" >}} and died without finding it.

She inherited her mother's maps, her mother's obsession, and her mother's particular inability to leave well enough alone. She has spent the four years since her mother's death following routes her mother marked but never finished, checking coastlines her mother sketched but never confirmed.

She does not know how she arrived in {{< term term="Aehr" >}}. She suspects the sea brought her, and that the sea had reasons.

## Personality

Sael is precise and observant in the way that people who were raised around maps tend to be: she notices distances, proportions, the shape of things relative to each other. She is also stubborn in a way that manifests as politeness — she will argue with you endlessly while remaining perfectly courteous throughout.

She is not especially brave. She is, however, very bad at stopping.

## Relationships

Her relationship with {{< char name="Veth" >}} begins as a transaction and becomes something more complicated, as things tend to do when one party is three hundred years dead and the other keeps asking questions.
